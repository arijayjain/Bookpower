What are the levels

Bookpower offers you special books. A is for Execlent for Execlent readers,B for good,C for Fair and N for Not So Good. In the A level we work on looking for tiny details use it to support your evidence. In B you work on not forgetting medium sized details like why was their a war. In C you look for common mistakes and solve them. Last in level N you learn basic reading and not miss big and easy details to catch and how to retell the story.
